## Overview

ScrollGyan is a free knowledge app that shows Wikipedia articles 
in Indian languages. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data We Collect

ScrollGyan does NOT collect any personal information.

- No login or account required
- No name, email, or phone number collected
- No location data collected
- No usage tracking or analytics
- No third-party advertising

## How the App Works

- Articles are fetched from Wikipedia's public API 
  (wikipedia.org) in real time
- Wikipedia's own privacy policy applies to those requests
- Saved articles are stored locally on your device only
- No data is sent to any server owned by ScrollGyan

## Third Party Services

ScrollGyan uses Wikipedia's public REST API to fetch articles.
Wikipedia is operated by the Wikimedia Foundation.
Their privacy policy is available at:
https://foundation.wikimedia.org/wiki/Privacy_policy

## Children

ScrollGyan is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect 
any information from children or adults.
