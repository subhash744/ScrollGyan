Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

ScrollGyan is a free knowledge app that shows Wikipedia articles in Indian languages. We are committed to protecting your privacy.


Data We Collect

ScrollGyan does NOT collect any personal information.

  • No login or account required
  • No name, email, or phone number collected
  • No location data collected
  • No usage tracking or analytics
  • No third-party advertising

How it Works

Articles are fetched from Wikipedia's public API in real time. Wikipedia's own privacy policy applies to those requests. Saved articles are stored locally on your device only. No data is sent to any server owned by ScrollGyan.
